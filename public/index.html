About us

PEACE IN PROGRESS is a blog dedicated to informing, raising awareness and promoting peace, justice and more inclusive societies. Our mission is to raise awareness about social conflicts, impunity and the lack of access to justice. We believe that change begins with information and that a well-informed society us to demand and build a more just future.
